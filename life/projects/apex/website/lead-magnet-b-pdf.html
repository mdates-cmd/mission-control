Prompt 1

Listing Description Generator

What it does

Turns basic property details into polished, MLS-ready copy with positioning, highlights, and buyer-facing language.

How to use it

Address, beds/baths, square footage, property type, standout features, area highlights, ideal buyer, and tone.

Prompt text

You are an expert real estate copywriter.

Write an MLS-ready listing description for the following property:
- Address: [INSERT ADDRESS]
- Beds/Baths: [INSERT BEDS/BATHS]
- Square Footage: [INSERT SQ FT]
- Property Type: [INSERT PROPERTY TYPE]
- Key Features: [INSERT FEATURES]
- Neighborhood or Area Highlights: [INSERT AREA DETAILS]
- Ideal Buyer: [INSERT TARGET BUYER]
- Tone: [professional / luxury / family-friendly / investor-focused]

Requirements:
1. Keep the description between 120 and 180 words.
2. Lead with the strongest selling angle.
3. Highlight the most valuable features naturally.
4. Avoid fair housing issues or prohibited language.
5. End with a compelling call to action.

Output:
- Headline option
- MLS description
- 3 alternate feature bullets for marketing use

Expected output

A headline, an MLS-ready description, and three alternate marketing bullets you can use immediately.

Prompt 2

Cold Outreach Email Writer

What it does

Writes a personalized seller outreach email based on lead info, motivation, and timeframe.

How to use it

Seller name, property address, lead type, motivation clues, timeframe, your value proposition, and tone.

Prompt text

You are writing a personalized real estate seller outreach email.

Use the following lead information:
- Seller Name: [INSERT NAME]
- Property Address: [INSERT ADDRESS]
- Lead Type: [absentee owner / inherited property / tired landlord / FSBO / pre-foreclosure / other]
- Motivation Clues: [INSERT DETAILS]
- Timeframe: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]
- My Offer or Value Proposition: [INSERT VALUE PROP]
- Tone: [professional / warm / direct]

Write an outreach email that:
1. Feels personal, not mass produced.
2. Acknowledges the seller's likely situation without sounding invasive.
3. Explains how I can help in a clear and low-pressure way.
4. Includes a strong but simple call to action.
5. Stays under 180 words.

Output:
- Subject line options (3)
- Main email draft
- Short follow-up variation if they do not reply

Expected output

Three subject lines, a main outreach email, and a short follow-up variation for no-response leads.

Prompt 3

30-Day Content Calendar Generator

What it does

Builds a full month of social and email content ideas in under two minutes.

How to use it

Market area, audience, primary goal, channels, brand voice, campaign focus, seasonal angles, and posting frequency.

Prompt text

You are a real estate content strategist creating a 30-day content calendar.

Use the following business details:
- Market Area: [INSERT AREA]
- Audience: [buyers / sellers / investors / landlords / mixed]
- Primary Goal: [lead generation / nurture / listings / authority / referrals]
- Content Channels: [Instagram / Facebook / email / blog / LinkedIn / other]
- Brand Voice: [professional / friendly / direct / educational]
- Current Offers or Focus: [INSERT CAMPAIGN OR SERVICE]
- Seasonal or Local Angles: [INSERT DETAILS]
- Content Frequency: [daily / weekdays only / 3x per week]

Build a 30-day content calendar that:
1. Mixes educational, promotional, trust-building, and engagement content.
2. Includes specific post or email ideas for each day.
3. Keeps the ideas practical and easy to execute.
4. Suggests a hook or subject line for each item.
5. Groups ideas in a way that supports consistent lead nurturing.

Output:
- 30-day calendar table or list
- Channel recommendation for each day
- Hook or subject line for each entry
- 5 quick CTA ideas I can reuse across the month

Expected output

A 30-day calendar with channel recommendations, hooks or subject lines, and reusable CTAs.

Prompt 4

Lead Follow-Up Sequence

What it does

Creates a three-touch follow-up sequence that feels useful, intentional, and human instead of pushy.

How to use it

Lead name, source, last contact date, interest type, context notes, and your desired tone.

Prompt text

You are building a 3-touch follow-up sequence for a real estate lead.

Lead details:
- Lead Name: [INSERT NAME]
- Lead Source: [website / Zillow / referral / Facebook / open house / other]
- Last Contact Date: [INSERT DATE]
- Lead Interest: [buying / selling / investing / renting / financing]
- Known Context: [INSERT NOTES]
- Desired Tone: [helpful / direct / consultative]

Create a 3-message follow-up sequence:
1. Message 1 should re-open the conversation.
2. Message 2 should add value or clarity.
3. Message 3 should create urgency or offer an easy next step.

Requirements:
- Keep each message under 90 words.
- Make the language natural and human.
- Do not sound desperate or generic.
- Include suggested send timing for each message.

Output:
- Touch 1
- Touch 2
- Touch 3
- Optional subject lines or text variations

Expected output

Three follow-up messages with timing guidance and optional subject lines or text variations.

Prompt 5

Market Update Generator

What it does

Converts local stats into a client-ready market summary that is informed, relevant, and easy to share.

How to use it

Market area, median sale price, days on market, inventory, monthly and yearly changes, notes, audience, and tone.

Prompt text

You are a real estate market analyst writing a client-ready local market update.

Use these local stats:
- Market Area: [INSERT AREA]
- Median Sale Price: [INSERT PRICE]
- Average Days on Market: [INSERT DOM]
- Inventory Level: [INSERT INVENTORY]
- Month-over-Month Change: [INSERT CHANGE]
- Year-over-Year Change: [INSERT CHANGE]
- Buyer or Seller Trend Notes: [INSERT NOTES]
- Audience: [buyers / sellers / investors / general audience]
- Tone: [professional / educational / concise]

Write a market update that:
1. Explains the data in plain English.
2. Highlights what changed and why it matters.
3. Gives the audience 2 or 3 practical takeaways.
4. Ends with a clear invitation to connect.

Output:
- Email version
- Short newsletter summary
- 3 social post hooks based on the update

Expected output

A full email version, a short newsletter summary, and three social hooks based on the update.
